Salsa Verde

This is one of my favorite recipes to make in my free time. I love to make this salsa as it goes great with a variety of different foods such as chips, tacos, eggs, tamales, burritos, and rice. With just the right amount of spice mixed in with a mouthful of flavor, you cannot go wrong with this recipe.

Ingredients used:

  • 10 tomatillos
  • 8 serranos
  • 2 habaneros
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 2 limes
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 table spoons of olive oil
  • 1/2 an onion
  • 5 garlic cloves

Supplies Required:

  • Blender
  • Cutting Board
  • Knife with a sharpenned blade

Directions

  1. First, prepare your worksurface by cleaning all counters that will be utilized and boiling a pot of water. Ensure that your hands are clean before proceeding.
  2. Next, peel the skin off of the garlic cloves and the tomatillos. Remove the pepper stems and mince half of an onion as well..
  3. When completed, wash all produce.
  4. At this point, you are ready to start boiling! Carefully submerge the tomatillos, peppers, garlic cloves, and onions into the water. Cook for approximately 10 minutes.
  5. While you wait for the ingredients to boil, chop the cilantro and peel the avocado.
  6. Finally, blend all ingredients mentioned previously along with 2 table spoons of olive oil. When the salsa is finished blending, add lime, salt, and pepper to taste.
  7. Provecho!
